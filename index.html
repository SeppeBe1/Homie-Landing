Group spirit

Group feeling is an important value within cohousing. Therefore, it is important that everyone feels good in the group and that you can have fun times together.

Organization

Good organization ensures a good experience within cohabitation. With a smooth division of labor and clear house rules, conflicts can be avoided and everyone can feel at home with a positive mindset.

Cost management
